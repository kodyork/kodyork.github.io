センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1993

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全17問)

1993年度 (本試験)

問1

Do you know how [ 49 ] in the evening the show will begin?

答え: 1. early
How early ...?「どれくらい早く…？」。時間。

問2

A book is not always a good book just [ 50 ] it is written by a famous writer.

答え: 1. because
not ... just because ~「単に〜だからといって…ではない」。

問3

I feel [ 51 ] and sleepy in Mr. Brown's lessons, since he is always using the same material.

答え: 1. bored
人が「退屈させられる」場合は bored。

問4

I went back to my hometown for the first time [ 52 ] ten years.

答え: 3. in
for the first time in + 期間「〜ぶりに初めて」。

問5

Although her parents had said "no" for a long time, they finally [ 53 ] her go to Europe alone.

答え: 3. let
let 人 原形不定詞「人に〜させてあげる」。

問6

I can't [ 54 ] that noise. It's driving me crazy.

答え: 3. stand
can't stand ~「〜を我慢できない」。

問7

I suggested to Mary [ 55 ] with me to collect empty cans on the street, but she said she was too busy.

答え: 2. that she come
suggest that S (should) 原形。

問8

Heavy industry is moving in the direction of more automated machines and [ 56 ] manpower.

答え: 3. less
manpower は不可算名詞なので less。

問9

You can use a large plastic bottle, [ 57 ] cut off, as a pot to grow young plants in.

答え: 4. with its top
with + O + C（過去分詞）の付帯状況。

問10

A large proportion of [ 58 ] English-speaking people watch on TV is of American origin.

答え: 2. what
関係代名詞 what を含む節が of の目的語。

問11

The heavy rainfall has caused vegetable prices to rise daily [ 59 ] the last two months.

答え: 2. for
for + 期間「〜の間」。

問12

you are [ 60 ] to gain weight in summer than in winter because you tend to lose your appetite when it is hot.

答え: 1. less likely
be likely to do。「夏は太りにくい」。

問13

[ 61 ] children the way she does, Sue should become a teacher.

答え: 3. liking
分詞構文。「〜なので」。

問14

I'll be surprised [ 62 ] an accident. He drives too fast.

答え: 1. if tom doesn't have
I'll be surprised if S doesn't ...「もし…しなければ驚きだ（＝きっと…するだろう）」。

問15

You live near us, don't you? Would you like to [ 63 ] our taxi?

答え: 4. share
share a taxi「タクシーを相乗りする」。

問16

More and more videos were being rented in Britain, yet the number of movie tickets sold [ 64 ] 81 percent from 53 to 96 million.

答え: 3. increased by
increase by ~「〜（差額）だけ増加する」。

問17

After six hours' climbing, we finally [ 65 ] the top of the mountain.

答え: 3. succeeded in reaching
succeed in doing「〜することに成功する」。
