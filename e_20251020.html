【20251020】朝から英作文！

問題

①彼女は調査部 (the research department) の人間です。

②転送していただいてもよろしいでしょうか？

解答

①She is in/from the research department. She works in the research department. She belongs to the research department.

②Could you please forward this? / Would you mind forwarding this (to me)? / Would it be possible for you to forward this?
