センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1996

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全15問)

1996年度 (本試験)

問1

“I really hope it'll rain tomorrow." "From [ 98 ] you just said, it seems you don't want to go on the picnic."

答え: 2. what
From what S V「SがVすることから判断すると」。

問2

"Do you know that Jean has got a new job?" "No, she [ 99 ] us nothing about it."

答え: 4. told
tell 人 物事 (SVOO)。

問3

“I saw Mr Yamada at Shinjuku Station this morning." "You [ 100 ] have. He's still on vacation in Hawaii."

答え: 1. couldn't
can't/couldn't have done「〜したはずがない」。

問4

“How's your father?" "He's fine. He's [ 101 ] to play tennis every Sunday."

答え: 3. still active enough
形容詞 + enough to do。still の位置。

問5

"What did he say?" "He asked me [ 102 ] him, and I accepted."

答え: 3. to marry
ask 人 to do。marry は他動詞。

問6

It's been a long time since we last met, [ 103 ]?

答え: 2. hasn't it
付加疑問文。It's been なので hasn't it?

問7

Many people criticized me, but I did what [ 104 ].

答え: 2. i thought was right
関係代名詞 what。I thought は挿入節。

問8

They've just become seniors, so they'll graduate [ 105 ] one more year.

答え: 3. in
in + 期間「（今から）〜後に」。

問9

I told them to stop talking, but they didn't pay [ 106 ].

答え: 2. any attention
pay attention。否定文なので any。

問10

This is the [ 107 ] best book I've ever read.

答え: 4. very
the very best。最上級の強調。

問11

He opened his mouth [ 108 ] to say "No," but he didn't.

答え: 2. as if
as if (he were) to say ...。「まるで〜するかのように」。

問12

We must keep in mind that smoking [ 109 ] us more harm than good.

答え: 2. does
do (人) harm。「害を与える」。

問13

A college education will [ 110 ] you to get a broader view of the world.

答え: 1. enable
enable 人 to do「人が〜することを可能にする」。

問14

I'm afraid your parents will never give their [ 111 ] to your trip abroad.

答え: 2. consent
give one's consent to ~「〜に同意を与える」。

問15

The people who watch a sporting event without taking part are called [ 112 ].

答え: 4. spectators
スポーツの「観客」。
