センター試験 文法語法クイズ 2009

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全10問)

2009年度 (本試験)

問1

“Did you know that Albert Einstein, the famous physicist, [ 247 ] meat?” “Yes, if my memory serves, he became a strict vegetarian one year before he died.”

答え: 4. stopped eating
過去の事実（亡くなる1年前に菜食主義者になった＝肉食をやめた）を述べているので、過去形 stop + 動名詞が適切。

問2

“I'd better call our neighbor to ask her to check the door of our apartment.” “You don't have to do that. I remember [ 248 ] it when we left.”

答え: 2. locking
remember doing で「（過去に）〜したことを覚えている」。remember to do は「忘れずに〜する」。出発した時に鍵をかけたことを覚えている。

問3

Hi! It's me. I'm sorry I'm late. I'm running [ 249 ] the direction of the ticket gate. I'll be with you in a minute.

答え: 1. in
in the direction of ~ で「〜の方向へ」。

問4

I don't think we can come up with a solution to the problem, however long we spend [ 250 ] it.

答え: 1. discussing
spend 時間 doing で「〜して時間を過ごす」。discuss は他動詞なので discussing it で良い。

問5

You've got [ 251 ] on your tie. Did you have fried eggs for breakfast?

答え: 3. some egg
ネクタイについているのは、調理された卵の一部（物質名詞）と考えられるため、不可算名詞扱いとなり some egg となる。

問6

“How about going to that conveyor-belt sushi bar again?” “I'm afraid we can't. It closed [ 252 ] last month.”

答え: 1. down
close down で「（店などが）閉鎖する、閉店する」。

問7

I arrived here early today because the traffic was [ 253 ] than usual.

答え: 3. lighter
早く到着できた理由として、交通量が「通常より少なかった」と考えるのが自然。heavy traffic (交通渋滞) の反対は light traffic。

問8

I've heard that in the U.S. smoking is [ 254 ] in public places such as restaurants or cafés. Is that true?

答え: 1. banned
ban は「（公式に）禁止する」。公共の場所での喫煙が禁止されている。

問9

You can choose either the train or the bus. If you want to save some time, the train would be better. The [ 255 ] is a little bit higher, though.

答え: 2. fare
fare は電車やバスなどの「運賃」。fine は罰金、interest は利子。

問10

“You never seem to gain weight! How do you stay so slim?” “Just lucky, I guess. It [ 256 ] in the family.”

答え: 3. runs
run in the family で「（性質・病気などが）家族に遺伝している」。
