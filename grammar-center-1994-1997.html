センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1994-1997

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全64問)

1994年度 (本試験)

問1 (66)

“No one was prepared for Professor Hill's questions." "I guess we [ 66 ] the lesson last night."

答え: 4. should have read
should have done で「〜すべきだったのに（しなかった）」。last night とあるので過去の行為に対する後悔を表す。

問2 (67)

“Which would you prefer, coffee or tea?" "[ 67 ] thank you."

答え: 1. Neither
どちらかを選ばずに「どちらもいらない」と答える場合、Neither (, thank you). と言う。

問3 (68)

"What's your favorite sport?" "[ 68 ]"

答え: 3. Skiing
「好きなスポーツは何ですか？」という問いには、動名詞 Skiing で答えるのが一般的。

問4 (69)

"That earthquake in Mexico happened a long time ago, didn't it?" "No, it was [ 69 ] last year."

答え: 2. as recently as
as recently as + 時期 で「つい〜（最近）に」。否定的なニュアンスを伴うことが多い。「昔ではなく、つい去年のことだ」。

問5 (70)

“Is Bill still using your car?" "Yes. I wonder when he [ 70 ] it."

答え: 4. will return
I wonder (when/what/if ...) のような思考・疑問を表す動詞に続く節の中では、未来のことであっても will を使うことができる。（時や条件を表す副詞節ではないため）

問6 (71)

The children were walking in a line with one teacher [ 71 ] and one behind.

答え: 3. in front
in front は副詞句で「前に」。in front of ~ は「〜の前に」という前置詞句。

問7 (72)

Cold chicken is delicious [ 72 ] salad.

答え: 2. when eaten with
when (it is) eaten with salad の (it is) が省略された形。チキンは食べられる側なので受動態。

問8 (73)

Today cars are so much [ 73 ] that we assume everyone has one.

答え: 3. taken for granted
take ~ for granted で「〜を当然のことと思う」。ここでは受動態で Cars are taken for granted 「車は当然のものと思われている」。

問9 (74)

That picture doesn't seem ugly to me; [ 74 ] I think it's rather beautiful.

答え: 3. on the contrary
on the contrary は「それどころか、むしろ」。前の文の内容を否定して、逆のことを言う時に使う。

問10 (75)

Nowadays not many students want to be teachers, probably because they find teachers salaries very [ 75 ].

答え: 4. low
給料（salaries）が「低い」は low を使う。cheap/expensive は物の値段に対して使うのが一般的。

問11 (76)

I have a strong objection [ 76 ] treated like this.

答え: 2. to being
object to ~ing で「〜することに反対する」。この to は前置詞なので、後ろは動名詞 (being)。

問12 (77)

Can you tell the difference between rice grown in Japan and [ 77 ]?

答え: 2. american rice
rice grown in Japan と American rice (アメリカ産の米) を比較している。rice は不可算名詞なので ones は使えない。

問13 (78)

The Brown lives in a [ 78 ] house.

答え: 1. big, white, two-story
複数の形容詞が名詞を修飾する場合、一般的に「大きさ→色→種類・材質」の順になることが多い。(a big white two-story house)

問14 (79)

Britain has recently has a high level of umemployment [ 79 ] many other countries.

答え: 4. of (?)
選択肢の構成に疑問があるが、文脈から "the same is true of ~" (〜にも同じことが当てはまる) の形を想定している可能性が高い。失業率が高いのは英国だけでなく、他の多くの国にも当てはまる、という意。

問15 (80)

I'm sorry to hear about your problem. But if you had taken my advice, you [ 80 ] in such trouble now.

答え: 4. wouldn't be
混合仮定法。If S had done ... (過去の事実に反する仮定), S would be ~ now (現在の事実に反する結果)。「もしあのアドバイスを聞いていたら、今頃そんな困っていないだろうに」。

問16 (81)

Tom is [ 81 ] a hard-working student than a mathematical genius; he always gets high scores in math without studying very hard.

答え: 2. more
more A than B で「BというよりむしろA」。後半の文から、彼は努力家というより天才だとわかる。

問17 (82)

[ 82 ] activities are those approved of by society because they are considered to be fair and honest.

答え: 2. Respectable
Respectable は「（社会的に）立派な、ちゃんとした」。Respective は「それぞれの」。

1995年度 (本試験)

問1 (83)

“This is my new dog. His name is Wisdom." "That's interesting. Why [ 83 ] did you give him such a name?

答え: 3. on earth
Why on earth ...? で疑問詞を強調し「一体全体なぜ…？」。

問2 (84)

“Can you [ 84 ] what that sign says?" "Yes. From where I'm standing it's quite clear. It says 'No Smoking.'"

答え: 3. make out
make out ~ で「〜を理解する、判読する」。

問3 (85)

“Do you like that song?" "Yes, I do. It always [ 85 ] me of my happy school days."

答え: 4. reminds
remind A of B で「AにBを思い出させる」。remember は「（人が）覚えている、思い出す」。

問4 (86)

“Was Jack at the party?" "I don't think so. If he had been, I [ 86 ] him."

答え: 4. would have seen
仮定法過去完了。If S had done ..., S would have done ...。「もし彼が来ていたら、私は彼に会っただろうに」。

問5 (87)

“Will you go shopping this afternoon?" "No, and [ 87 ]."

答え: 3. neither will mary
前の否定文を受けて「〜もまた…ない」と言う場合は、Neither/Nor + 助動詞 + S の倒置形を使う。

問6 (88)

“You're not afraid of insects, are you?" "No, certainly not. Not flies, not mosquitoes, and [ 88 ] ants."

答え: 4. least of all
least of all で「とりわけ〜ない」。否定文の後で使う。

問7 (89)

It's [ 89 ] a long time since I started to teach at this school.

答え: 3. quite
quite a long time で「かなり長い間」。pretty も副詞だが、 a の前に置くのが普通 (a pretty long time)。

問8 (90)

She is so [ 90 ] that she cried for days when her pet rabbit died.

答え: 4. sensitive
sensitive は「感受性の強い、敏感な」。sensible は「分別のある、賢明な」。

問9 (91)

We have [ 91 ] to walk before sunset.

答え: 2. another ten miles
another + 数詞 + 複数名詞 で「さらに〜（数）の…」。

問10 (92)

There are few places downtown for parking, [ 92 ] is really a problem.

答え: 3. which
関係代名詞 which の非制限用法。先行詞は前の文全体（駐車場が少ないこと）。

問11 (93)

I liked his new house, but I hadn't expected it [ 93 ] so small.

答え: 3. to be
expect O to be C で「OがCであると予期する」。

問12 (94)

[ 94 ] fashion, I know nothing.

答え: 2. When it comes to
When it comes to ~ で「〜のこととなると」。

問13 (95)

You will find the word "psychology" [ 95 ] under "P" in your dictionary.

答え: 3. listed
find O C (過去分詞) で「Oが〜されているのを見つける」。単語はリストされる側なので過去分詞。

問14 (96)

Our doctor always talks to me [ 96 ] a teacher talking to a child.

答え: 2. like
like A doing ... で「…するAのように」。ここでは前置詞の like が適切。as if S V でも表現できるが、選択肢にない。

問15 (97)

The word [ 97 ] means money to be paid by people or businesses to a government for public purposes.

答え: 3. tax
政府に支払うお金は「税金 (tax)」。Income (収入)、Salary (給料:月給)、Wage (賃金:時給/日給) は不適切。

1996年度 (本試験)

問1 (98)

“I really hope it'll rain tomorrow." "From [ 98 ] you just said, it seems you don't want to go on the picnic."

答え: 2. what
From what S V で「SがVすることから判断すると」。関係代名詞 what が from の目的語になる名詞節を作る。

問2 (99)

"Do you know that Jean has got a new job?" "No, she [ 99 ] us nothing about it."

答え: 4. told
tell 人 物事 (SVOO) の形。「私たちに何も言わなかった」。say は say 物事 (to 人)。

問3 (100)

“I saw Mr Yamada at Shinjuku Station this morning." "You [ 100 ] have. He's still on vacation in Hawaii."

答え: 1. couldn't
can't/couldn't have done で「〜したはずがない」。過去の出来事に対する強い否定の推量。

問4 (101)

“How's your father?" "He's fine. He's [ 101 ] to play tennis every Sunday."

答え: 3. still active enough
形容詞/副詞 + enough to do で「〜するのに十分…」。still は副詞で通常 be動詞の後、形容詞の前に置かれる。

問5 (102)

"What did he say?" "He asked me [ 102 ] him, and I accepted."

答え: 3. to marry
ask 人 to do で「人に〜するように頼む」。marry は他動詞なので前置詞は不要。

問6 (103)

It's been a long time since we last met, [ 103 ]?

答え: 2. hasn't it
付加疑問文。主節の動詞が has been なので、hasn't it? となる。

問7 (104)

Many people criticized me, but I did what [ 104 ].

答え: 2. i thought was right
関係代名詞 what を含む節。what が was right の主語、かつ did の目的語になっている。「自分が正しいと思ったこと」。I thought は挿入節。

問8 (105)

They've just become seniors, so they'll graduate [ 105 ] one more year.

答え: 3. in
in + 期間 で「（今から）〜後に」。未来の時を表す。

問9 (106)

I told them to stop talking, but they didn't pay [ 106 ].

答え: 2. any attention
pay attention で「注意を払う」。attention は不可算名詞。否定文なので any を使う。

問10 (107)

This is the [ 107 ] best book I've ever read.

答え: 4. very
the very best で最上級を強調し「まさに最高の」。

問11 (108)

He opened his mouth [ 108 ] to say "No," but he didn't.

答え: 2. as if
as if (he were) to say "No" の (he were) が省略された形。「まるでノーと言うかのように」口を開けた。

問12 (109)

We must keep in mind that smoking [ 109 ] us more harm than good.

答え: 2. does
do (人) harm で「（人）に害を与える」。do (人) more harm than good で「（人）にとって益より害の方が多い」。

問13 (110)

A college education will [ 110 ] you to get a broader view of the world.

答え: 1. enable
enable 人 to do で「人が〜することを可能にする」。

問14 (111)

I'm afraid your parents will never give their [ 111 ] to your trip abroad.

答え: 2. consent
give one's consent to ~ で「〜に同意（承諾）を与える」。

問15 (112)

The people who watch a sporting event without taking part are called [ 112 ].

答え: 4. spectators
spectator(s) でスポーツなどの「観客」。audience は演劇やコンサートの観客。

1997年度 (本試験)

問1 (113)

“He's a good skier, isn't he?" "Yes, he really is. I wish I [ 113 ] like him."

答え: 2. could ski
I wish + 仮定法過去 (S' + 過去形...) で「（現在）〜だったらなあ」。現在の事実に反する願望。

問2 (114)

“Everything you cook tastes really good." "Thanks, but I don't think I'm [ 114 ] cook as you."

答え: 3. as good a
as 形容詞 a/an 名詞 as ~ で「〜と同じくらい…な（名詞）」。語順に注意。

問3 (115)

“Is English spoken in Japan?" "Well, [ 115 ] Japanese people don't use English in everyday life."

答え: 3. most
most Japanese people で「ほとんどの日本人」。almost は副詞なので almost all Japanese people なら可。

問4 (116)

“Do you know what Junko is doing?" "Well, she [ 116 ] a book when I saw her a while ago."

答え: 4. was reading
when I saw her (過去) の時点で「本を読んでいた」ので、過去進行形が適切。

問5 (117)

"Are you going somewhere during the vacation?" "Yes, I've found a nice beach [ 117 ] I can enjoy swimming even in February."

答え: 3. where
先行詞 a nice beach を場所として修飾する関係副詞 where。

問6 (118)

“It's strange that Jane hasn't come yet." "Yes, she hasn't missed a single meeting so [ 118 ]."

答え: 1. far
so far で「今までのところ」。現在完了形と共に用いられることが多い。

問7 (119)

Our teacher says computers will never take [ 119 ] of books.

答え: 4. the place
take the place of ~ で「〜に取って代わる」。

問8 (120)

You ought to [ 120 ] than to go to such a dangerous place.

答え: 2. know better
know better than to do で「〜するほど愚かではない、〜しないだけの分別がある」。

問9 (121)

It's been years since I asked you [ 121 ] any help.

答え: 1. for
ask (人) for ~ で「（人）に〜を求める、頼む」。

問10 (122)

John has been [ 122 ] the ID card he lost yesterday.

答え: 3. looking for
look for ~ で「〜を探す」。search は search A for B (Bを求めてAを探す) のように使うことが多い。

問11 (123)

This country is so [ 123 ] that it takes no more than a day to drive around it.

答え: 3. small
so small that ...「とても小さいので…」。1日で一周できるほど小さい国、という文脈。

問12 (124)

It's not very important. We might [ 124 ] well forget about it.

答え: 1. as
might as well do で「〜するほうがよいだろう、〜してもいいだろう」。

問13 (125)

There are signs [ 125 ] the economy is improving at last.

答え: 3. that
同格のthat節。signs = the economy is improving という内容。「経済がついに改善しているという兆候」。

問14 (126)

Do you know what has [ 126 ] of the actor who was so popular ten years ago?

答え: 1. become
what has become of ~? で「〜はどうなったのか？」と消息を尋ねる表現。
