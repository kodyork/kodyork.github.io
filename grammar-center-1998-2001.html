センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1998-2001

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全52問)

1998年度 (本試験)

問1 (127)

“What's that building?" "That's a movie theater. They [ 130 ] a new movie there every week."

答え: 3. show
映画館で映画を「上映する」は show a movie。

問2 (128)

“Have you finished writing your report?" "No, but I'm [ 131 ]."

答え: 2. almost finished
I'm almost finished. で「もうすぐ終わります」。finished は形容詞的に使われている。

問3 (129)

“May I speak to Mr. Davis?" "I'm sorry, he [ 132 ] a meeting right now."

答え: 3. is attending
right now (今ちょうど) があるので、現在進行形が適切。「会議に出席中です」。

問4 (130)

“I have a terrible toothache." "You [ 133 ] go to the dentist's."

答え: 1. had better
had better + 原形不定詞で「〜したほうがよい」。やや強い忠告。

問5 (131)

“I don't like horror movies." "[ 134 ]. I like comedies better."

答え: 3. Me, neither
相手の否定文に対して「私も〜ない」と同意する場合、Me, neither. や Neither do I. を使う。

問6 (132)

“How about going to the concert with me on Saturday night?" "I'd love to. But [ 135 ] I have to work."

答え: 2. I'm afraid
I'm afraid (that) ... で「残念ながら…だと思う」。断る際の丁寧な表現。

問7 (133)

“What time are you going to [ 136 ] for the airport tomorrow?" "About seven in the morning."

答え: 3. leave
leave for ~ で「〜へ向けて出発する」。depart はやや硬い表現。go to ~ / reach ~。

問8 (134)

My uncle is going to stay [ 137 ] a week.

答え: 1. at least
at least で「少なくとも」。at most は「多くても」。at last は「ついに」。

問9 (135)

I found [ 138 ] to understand what the foreign speaker was saying.

答え: 1. it difficult
find it C to do で「〜することはCだとわかる/思う」。it は形式目的語で、真の目的語は to understand 以下。

問10 (136)

My sister is [ 139 ] for her wedding.

答え: 2. busy preparing
be busy doing で「〜するのに忙しい」。

問11 (137)

After the accident, the trains were [ 140 ] schedule by more than an hour.

答え: 1. behind
behind schedule で「予定より遅れて」。↔ on schedule 「予定通りに」。

問12 (138)

I read the book, but I didn't like it. [ 141 ], it was rather boring.

答え: 2. As a matter of fact
As a matter of fact で「実際のところ、実は」。前の文の内容を補足・強調する。

問13 (139)

“My daughter will be in Tokyo next week." "Really? I'll be glad [ 142 ] her."

答え: 3. to meet
be glad to do で「〜して嬉しい」。感情の原因を表す不定詞の副詞的用法。

問14 (140)

I was [ 143 ] when I heard the news that the famous actor had died.

答え: 2. shocked
人が「ショックを受ける」場合は shocked (過去分詞)。shocking は「衝撃的な」（ニュースなど）。

1999年度 (本試験)

問1 (141)

“I don't see Tom. I wonder why he's late." "Well, he [ 144 ] his train, or maybe he overslept."

答え: 1. might have missed
might have done で「〜したかもしれない」。過去の出来事に対する推量。

問2 (142)

“Could you tell me how to get to the city library?" "Sure. Go straight for two blocks, and you'll see it [ 145 ]."

答え: 2. on your right
「あなたの右側に」は on your right (side)。↔ on your left。

問3 (143)

“Is it all right if I use this telephone?" "Yes, of course. [ 146 ]."

答え: 1. Be my guest
Be my guest. は「どうぞご自由に（お使いください）」。許可を与える際のくだけた表現。

問4 (144)

"What a beautiful picture! Did you paint it yourself?" "No, I [ 147 ]."

答え: 1. had it painted
have O 過去分詞 で「Oを〜してもらう（使役）」。ここでは「（誰かに）それを描いてもらった」。

問5 (145)

"Why don't you sing this song?" "I don't like it. It [ 148 ] me of an old love affair."

答え: 3. reminds
remind A of B で「AにBを思い出させる」。

問6 (146)

My father is a good golfer, but he is [ 149 ] satisfied with his performance.

答え: 3. seldom
seldom は「めったに〜ない」という頻度を表す副詞（準否定語）。文脈（but 以下）に合う。

問7 (147)

I don't know the reason why she avoids [ 150 ] me.

答え: 1. seeing
avoid は動名詞 (doing) を目的語にとる動詞。「私に会うのを避ける」。

問8 (148)

A new bridge is [ 151 ] construction.

答え: 4. under
under construction で「建設中」。

問9 (149)

In Japan, [ 152 ] April, many colleges and companies welcome their new students and employees.

答え: 3. in
月や年、季節の前には前置詞 in を使う (in April)。

問10 (150)

My grandfather is eighty years old and he is [ 153 ] strong.

答え: 2. still
still は「まだ、依然として」。80歳だが、まだ丈夫だ、という文脈。

問11 (151)

Ken [ 154 ] the teacher about the date of the examination.

答え: 1. asked
ask (人) about ~ で「（人）に〜について尋ねる」。

問12 (152)

I want this letter [ 155 ] immediately.

答え: 4. to be delivered
want O to be done で「Oが〜されることを望む」。手紙は配達される側なので受動態の不定詞。

2000年度 (本試験)

問1 (153)

“Does your father always drive you to the station?" "No, not always. [ 156 ]."

答え: 1. But sometimes he does
sometimes は文頭、be動詞/助動詞の後、一般動詞の前に置かれることが多い。does は drives me to the station を受ける代動詞。

問2 (154)

"What time does the movie start?" "I'm not sure. I'll [ 157 ]."

答え: 1. find it out
find out (調べる) の目的語が代名詞(it)の場合、find it out の語順になる。

問3 (155)

“I passed the entrance examination!" "[ 158 ]"

答え: 1. Congratulations!
合格を祝う言葉。「おめでとう！」。

問4 (156)

“Could you do me a favor?" "[ 159 ]"

答え: 3. Sure, what is it?
「お願いがあるのですが」「もちろん、何ですか？」という自然な会話の流れ。

問5 (157)

“I'd like to make an appointment to see Dr. Smith." "Well, he's usually available [ 160 ] two and four in the afternoon."

答え: 2. between
between A and B で「AとBの間に」。ここでは午後2時から4時の間。

問6 (158)

“It was very kind [ 161 ] you to help me the other day." "Don't mention it."

答え: 2. of
It is 形容詞 of 人 to do ... の形で、形容詞が人の性質を表す場合(kind, nice, stupidなど)は of を使う。

問7 (159)

“Haven't you finished that report yet?" "[ 162 ] me a few more minutes and I'll get it done."

答え: 1. Give
Give me a few more minutes. で「あと数分ください」。命令文。

問8 (160)

“Our trip to Tokyo was fun, wasn't it?" "Yes, it was great! I'm really looking forward [ 163 ] there again sometime."

答え: 4. to going
look forward to ~ing で「〜するのを楽しみに待つ」。この to は前置詞。

問9 (161)

“Is that Italian restaurant next to the bookstore new?" "No, I think [ 164 ] for more than a year now."

答え: 1. it's been open
for more than a year (1年以上) という期間があるので、現在完了形。「開店してから1年以上になる」。open はここでは形容詞。

問10 (162)

“May I use your phone?" "Sure, [ 165 ]. I'm not expecting any calls right now."

答え: 2. go ahead
Go ahead. は許可を与えるときの「どうぞ」。

問11 (163)

It's a [ 166 ] your wife couldn't come. I really wanted to meet her.

答え: 2. shame
It's a shame (that) ... で「…なのは残念だ」。= It's a pity (that) ...。

問12 (164)

He [ 167 ] have written the letter, because he doesn't know how to write.

答え: 1. can't
can't have done で「〜したはずがない」。過去の出来事に対する強い否定の推量。「彼は字が書けないのだから、その手紙を書いたはずがない」。

2001年度 (本試験)

問1 (165)

If it [ 168 ] raining soon, shall we go out for a walk?

答え: 2. stops
時や条件を表す副詞節 (If ...) の中では、未来のことであっても現在形を使う。

問2 (166)

At first no one in class could find an answer, but finally David came up [ 169 ] one.

答え: 4. with
come up with ~ で「〜を思いつく」。

問3 (167)

Jim had a skiing accident yesterday, but he's all right. He's lucky, because he [ 170 ] hurt himself badly.

答え: 1. could have
could have done で「〜した可能性があった（が、実際はしなかった）」。大怪我をした可能性があったが、幸運にも大丈夫だった、という文脈。

問4 (168)

Something's wrong with the car! We must have a [ 171 ] tire.

答え: 3. flat
a flat tire で「パンクしたタイヤ」。

問5 (169)

“You are coming with us to the beach on Sunday, aren't you?" "I'm really sorry, but I won't be able to [ 172 ] it after all."

答え: 2. make
make it で「（予定などに）間に合う、都合をつける、参加できる」。ここでは「参加できそうにない」。

問6 (170)

A [ 173 ] is the money that you pay for a journey made, for example, by bus, train, or taxi.

答え: 3. fare
バス、電車、タクシーなどの「運賃」は fare。fee は専門家への謝礼や入場料など。charge はサービス料。cost は費用全般。

問7 (171)

“Do you need that report right away?" "No, there's no hurry. Take your [ 174 ]."

答え: 4. time
Take your time. で「ごゆっくりどうぞ、急がなくていいですよ」。

問8 (172)

Can you [ 175 ] the noise from the apartment next door?

答え: 2. stand
stand ~ で「〜を我慢する」。否定文で使われることが多い (Can't stand ~)。

問9 (173)

My computer is [ 176 ] to yours: it's the same make and model.

答え: 1. identical
identical to ~ で「〜と全く同じである」。same は the same as ~。similar to ~ は「似ている」。

問10 (174)

He has three cars, and each of them [ 177 ] a different purpose.

答え: 1. serves
serve a purpose で「目的にかなう、役立つ」。each of them が主語なので単数扱い。
