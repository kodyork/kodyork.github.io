センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1998-2001

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全47問)

1998年度 (本試験)

問1

“What's that building?" "That's a movie theater. They [ 127 ] a new movie there every week."

答え: 3. show
映画館で映画を「上映する」は show a movie。

問2

“Have you finished writing your report?" "No, but I'm [ 128 ]."

答え: 2. almost finished
I'm almost finished. で「もうすぐ終わります」。

問3

“May I speak to Mr. Davis?" "I'm sorry, he [ 129 ] a meeting right now."

答え: 3. is attending
right now があるので現在進行形。

問4

“I have a terrible toothache." "You [ 130 ] go to the dentist's."

答え: 1. had better
had better + 原形不定詞。「〜したほうがよい」。

問5

“I don't like horror movies." "[ 131 ]. I like comedies better."

答え: 3. me, neither
否定文への同意。「私も〜ない」。

問6

“How about going to the concert with me on Saturday night?" "I'd love to. But [ 132 ] I have to work."

答え: 2. i'm afraid
I'm afraid (that) ...「残念ながら…だと思う」。

問7

“What time are you going to [ 133 ] for the airport tomorrow?" "About seven in the morning."

答え: 3. leave
leave for ~ 「〜へ向けて出発する」。

問8

My uncle is going to stay [ 134 ] a week.

答え: 1. at least
at least「少なくとも」。

問9

I found [ 135 ] to understand what the foreign speaker was saying.

答え: 1. it difficult
find it C to do。it は形式目的語。

問10

My sister is [ 136 ] for her wedding.

答え: 2. busy preparing
be busy doing「〜するのに忙しい」。

問11

After the accident, the trains were [ 137 ] schedule by more than an hour.

答え: 1. behind
behind schedule「予定より遅れて」。

問12

I read the book, but I didn't like it. [ 138 ], it was rather boring.

答え: 2. As a matter of fact
As a matter of fact「実際のところ」。

問13

“My daughter will be in Tokyo next week." "Really? I'll be glad [ 139 ] her."

答え: 3. to meet
be glad to do「〜して嬉しい」。

問14

I was [ 140 ] when I heard the news that the famous actor had died.

答え: 2. shocked
人が「ショックを受ける」場合は shocked。

1999年度 (本試験)

問1 (141)

“I don't see Tom. I wonder why he's late." "Well, he [ 141 ] his train, or maybe he overslept."

答え: 1. might have missed
might have done「〜したかもしれない」。過去の推量。

問2 (142)

“Could you tell me how to get to the city library?" "Sure. Go straight for two blocks, and you'll see it [ 142 ]."

答え: 2. on your right
「あなたの右側に」。

問3 (143)

“Is it all right if I use this telephone?" "Yes, of course. [ 143 ]."

答え: 1. Be my guest
「どうぞご自由に」。許可を与える表現。

問4 (144)

"What a beautiful picture! Did you paint it yourself?" "No, I [ 144 ]."

答え: 1. had it painted
have O 過去分詞「Oを〜してもらう」。

問5 (145)

"Why don't you sing this song?" "I don't like it. It [ 145 ] me of an old love affair."

答え: 3. reminds
remind A of B「AにBを思い出させる」。

問6 (146)

My father is a good golfer, but he is [ 146 ] satisfied with his performance.

答え: 3. seldom
seldom「めったに〜ない」。

問7 (147)

I don't know the reason why she avoids [ 147 ] me.

答え: 1. seeing
avoid doing。

問8 (148)

A new bridge is [ 148 ] construction.

答え: 4. under
under construction「建設中」。

問9 (149)

In Japan, [ 149 ] April, many colleges and companies welcome their new students and employees.

答え: 3. in
月の前は in。

問10 (150)

My grandfather is eighty years old and he is [ 150 ] strong.

答え: 2. still
still「まだ」。

問11 (151)

Ken [ 151 ] the teacher about the date of the examination.

答え: 1. asked
ask (人) about ~「〜について尋ねる」。

問12 (152)

I want this letter [ 152 ] immediately.

答え: 4. to be delivered
want O to be done「Oが〜されることを望む」。

2000年度 (本試験)

問1 (153)

“Does your father always drive you to the station?" "No, not always. [ 153 ]."

答え: 1. But sometimes he does
sometimes の位置と代動詞 does。

問2 (154)

"What time does the movie start?" "I'm not sure. I'll [ 154 ]."

答え: 1. find it out
find out の目的語が代名詞(it)の場合、find it out。

問3 (155)

“I passed the entrance examination!" "[ 155 ]"

答え: 1. Congratulations!
合格祝い。「おめでとう！」。

問4 (156)

“Could you do me a favor?" "[ 156 ]"

答え: 3. Sure, what is it?
お願いに対する返答。「もちろん、何ですか？」。

問5 (157)

“I'd like to make an appointment to see Dr. Smith." "Well, he's usually available [ 157 ] two and four in the afternoon."

答え: 2. between
between A and B「AとBの間に」。

問6 (158)

“It was very kind [ 158 ] you to help me the other day." "Don't mention it."

答え: 2. of
It is 形容詞 of 人 to do ... （人の性質）。

問7 (159)

“Haven't you finished that report yet?" "[ 159 ] me a few more minutes and I'll get it done."

答え: 1. Give
Give me ...「〜ください」。

問8 (160)

“Our trip to Tokyo was fun, wasn't it?" "Yes, it was great! I'm really looking forward [ 160 ] there again sometime."

答え: 4. to going
look forward to doing「〜するのを楽しみに待つ」。to は前置詞。

問9 (161)

“Is that Italian restaurant next to the bookstore new?" "No, I think [ 161 ] for more than a year now."

答え: 1. it's been open
for + 期間があるので現在完了形。open は形容詞。

問10 (162)

“May I use your phone?" "Sure, [ 162 ]. I'm not expecting any calls right now."

答え: 2. go ahead
Go ahead.「どうぞ」。

問11 (163)

It's a [ 163 ] your wife couldn't come. I really wanted to meet her.

答え: 2. shame
It's a shame (that) ... 「…なのは残念だ」。

2001年度 (本試験)

問1 (165)

If it [ 165 ] raining soon, shall we go out for a walk?

答え: 2. stops
条件を表す副詞節の中では未来のことでも現在形。

問2 (166)

At first no one in class could find an answer, but finally David came up [ 166 ] one.

答え: 4. with
come up with ~「〜を思いつく」。

問3 (167)

Jim had a skiing accident yesterday, but he's all right. He's lucky, because he [ 167 ] hurt himself badly.

答え: 1. could have
could have done「〜した可能性があった」。実際にはしなかった。

問4 (168)

Something's wrong with the car! We must have a [ 168 ] tire.

答え: 3. flat
a flat tire「パンクしたタイヤ」。

問5 (169)

“You are coming with us to the beach on Sunday, aren't you?" "I'm really sorry, but I won't be able to [ 169 ] it after all."

答え: 2. make
make it「都合をつける、参加できる」。

問6 (170)

A [ 170 ] is the money that you pay for a journey made, for example, by bus, train, or taxi.

答え: 3. fare
交通機関の「運賃」。

問7 (171)

“Do you need that report right away?" "No, there's no hurry. Take your [ 171 ]."

答え: 4. time
Take your time.「ごゆっくりどうぞ」。

問8 (173)

We [ 173 ] playing baseball for about half an hour when it started to rain heavily.

答え: 1. had been
過去のある時点まで継続していた動作は過去完了進行形。

問9 (174)

"Mom, can I use the car tonight?" "No, that's [ 174 ]. Remember what happened last time!"

答え: 3. out of the question
「問題外だ、不可能だ」。強い拒絶。

問10 (175)

"We had terrible weather during our vacation." "[ 175 ]? What a shame!"

答え: 1. Did you
前の文 We had ... (過去形) を受けて「そうだったの？」。
