【20251025】朝から英作文！

問題

① 会議の主な論点は何でしたか？

② 議事録が準備でき次第、送っていただけますか？

解答

① What were the main points/ key points discussed in the meeting? (main topicsもOK、cf., main agenda items)

② Could you send me the minutes as soon as they are ready/ available/ prepared? (once/when they are…などOK) after preparation も意味は通じるが、急ぐ感じはしない
