センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1990-1993

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全65問)

1990年度 (本試験)

問1

Look! There's a dog in the hall. Someone must have left the door [ 1 ].

答え: 2. open
leave O C (OをCの状態にしておく) の形。door が open な状態なので、形容詞の open が入る。

問2

Each of the sumo wrestlers [ 2 ] over 100 kg.

答え: 3. weighs
Each of ~ が主語なので単数扱い。動詞「重さがある」は weigh。三単現のsが必要。

問3

After a lot of problems she [ 3 ] to learn to drive a car.

答え: 2. managed
manage to do で「なんとか〜する」。succeed は succeed in doing の形をとる。

問4

I couldn't meet him at the station because my car ran out of [ 4 ].

答え: 1. gas
run out of ~ で「〜を使い果たす」。文脈から「ガソリン」が適切。

問5

My TV set is almost 15 years old, but it still has a good [ 5 ].

答え: 3. picture
have a good picture でテレビの「映りが良い」。

問6

This river is dangerous to [ 6 ] in July.

答え: 2. swim in
It is dangerous to swim in this river. の this river が主語になった形。前置詞 in が必要。

問7

[ 7 ] his many hours of study, he did not make much progress in German.

答え: 3. despite
「〜にもかかわらず」の意味を持つ前置詞。Despite = In spite of。

問8

John and Mary [ 8 ] each other since 1976.

答え: 2. have known
know は状態動詞なので進行形にしない。since があるので現在完了形。

問9

His mother [ 9 ] to be more careful in his choice of words.

答え: 1. advised him
advise 人 to do で「人に〜するように忠告する」。

問10

The Olympic Games were a huge success because so many countries [ 10 ].

答え: 1. participated
participate (in ~) で「〜に参加する」。自動詞なので目的語は不要。

問11

Where in Australia [ 11 ]?

答え: 1. did you grow up
grow up は自動詞で「育つ」。raise は他動詞で「〜を育てる」。

問12

Some people fear that World War III may break out soon, but I [ 12 ].

答え: 2. doubt if it will
doubt if/whether ~ で「〜かどうか疑う」。ここでは「そうは思わない」の意。

問13

If you buy this, I will give you a [ 13 ].

答え: 1. fifteen percent discount
数値+percent が形容詞的に discount を修飾する。percent は複数形にしない。

問14

Jeff and Jenny saved [ 14 ] they could to visit their uncle in Hawaii.

答え: 2. as much money as
as much 名詞 as S can で「Sができるだけ多くの（名詞）」。money は不可算名詞なので much を使う。

1991年度 (本試験)

問1 (15)

Does Kenji still have that book he [ 15 ] from the library?

答え: 2. borrowed
borrow A from B で「BからAを（無料で）借りる」。lend は「貸す」、rent は「（有料で）借りる」。

問2 (16)

His wife is in the hospital because she was [ 16 ] in a car crash.

答え: 4. injured
事故などで人が「怪我をする」場合は injure を使う。damage や destroy は主に物に対して使う。

問3 (17)

I could tell he was only [ 17 ] to read, because his book was upside down.

答え: 4. pretending
pretend to do で「〜するふりをする」。本が逆さまなので、読んでいるふりをしていたとわかる。

問4 (18)

Before the exam I [ 18 ] my memory by reading some old notes.

答え: 2. refreshed
refresh one's memory で「記憶を新たにする、思い出させる」。

問5 (19)

When I missed the last bus, I had no [ 19 ] but to take a taxi.

答え: 1. choice
have no choice but to do で「〜するしかない、〜するより他に選択肢がない」。

問6 (20)

In spite of the [ 20 ] traffic, I managed to get to the airport in time.

答え: 1. heavy
heavy traffic で「交通渋滞」。In spite of ~ (〜にもかかわらず) があるので、文脈に合う。

問7 (21)

I don't know what this word means. I'll look it [ 21 ] in the dictionary.

答え: 4. up
look up A (in B) で「Aを（Bで）調べる」。

問8 (22)

We'll have to move that big desk; it's really [ 22 ] the way.

答え: 2. in
be in the way で「邪魔になっている」。

問9 (23)

Let's ask the neighbors to [ 23 ] the dog while we're away.

答え: 3. look after
look after ~ で「〜の世話をする」。

問10 (24)

I've already had one bad experience buying goods by mail order and I don't want [ 24 ].

答え: 1. another
another = an other (experience)。「もう一つの（悪い経験）」はしたくない、の意。

問11 (25)

I'd rather have a room of my own, [ 25 ] small it may be.

答え: 1. however
however 形容詞/副詞 S may be で「どんなに〜であっても」。譲歩を表す。

問12 (26)

[ 26 ] seems easy at first often turns out to be difficult.

答え: 3. what
関係代名詞 what を含む節 (What seems easy at first) が文全体の主語になっている。「最初は簡単に思えること」の意。

問13 (27)

The boys should not [ 27 ] the ice cream, but they did.

答え: 4. have eaten
should not have done で「〜すべきではなかったのに（してしまった）」。過去の行為に対する後悔や非難を表す。

問14 (28)

I'm having my house [ 28 ] at the moment, so could you come over next Friday instead?

答え: 3. painted
have O 過去分詞 で「Oを〜してもらう（使役）」または「Oを〜される（被害）」。ここでは「家をペンキ塗りしてもらっている」。

問15 (29)

This symphony is a real masterpiece. I think it's worth [ 29 ] over and over again.

答え: 2. listening to
be worth doing で「〜する価値がある」。listen to ~ なので、to が必要。

問16 (30)

The boy opened the window, although his mother told him [ 30 ].

答え: 3. not to
tell 人 not to do で「人に〜しないように言う」。to 以下 (open the window) が省略されている。

問17 (31)

My wife usually doesn't drink coffee at night, and [ 31 ].

答え: 3. neither do i
前の否定文を受けて「〜もまた…ない」と言う場合は、Neither/Nor + 助動詞 + S の倒置形を使う。

1992年度 (本試験)

問1 (32)

What is he talkig about? It just doesn't make [ 32 ].

答え: 3. sense
make sense で「意味をなす、理解できる」。doesn't make sense で「意味不明だ」。

問2 (33)

Susan gave up her job, so she has a lot of time to [ 33 ].

答え: 4. spare
time to spare で「自由に使える時間」。

問3 (34)

She had a dream of becoming a movie star even though she didn't know how to [ 34 ] it.

答え: 4. realize
realize a dream で「夢を実現する」。

問4 (35)

It's only a small house but it [ 35 ] my needs perfectly.

答え: 2. meets
meet one's needs で「（人）の要求（ニーズ）を満たす」。

問5 (36)

Could you lend me some money? I'm [ 36 ] of cash at the moment.

答え: 4. short
be short of ~ で「〜が不足している」。

問6 (37)

I've lived near the airport so long that I'm now [ 37 ] to the noise of the airplanes.

答え: 4. used
be used to ~ で「〜に慣れている」。

問7 (38)

[ 38 ] your financial help, we wouldn't be able to carry out our plan.

答え: 4. without
Without ~ (, S would ...) で「もし〜がなければ、Sは…だろうに」。仮定法過去の表現。

問8 (39)

[ 39 ] taking the regular tests, we have to hand in a long essay.

答え: 4. in addition to
in addition to ~ で「〜に加えて」。= besides。

問9 (40)

I just can't see why he doesn't [ 40 ] his motorcycle, since he never rides it any more.

答え: 4. get rid of
get rid of ~ で「〜を処分する、捨てる」。

問10 (41)

There are two reasons for our decision, and you know one of them. Now I'll tell you [ 41 ].

答え: 3. the other
2つのうちの一方を one、もう一方を the other と言う。

問11 (42)

The firemen had trouble getting to the street [ 42 ] the houses were on fire.

答え: 3. where
先行詞 the street を場所として修飾する関係副詞 where。

問12 (43)

Remember [ 43 ] I've just told you. It'll be very important when you grow up.

答え: 3. what
Remember の目的語となる名詞節を作る関係代名詞 what。「私が今言ったこと」の意。

問13 (44)

My parents never allowed me [ 44 ] alone in the sea.

答え: 3. to swim
allow 人 to do で「人が〜することを許す」。

問14 (45)

Kate speaks English very fast. I've never heard English [ 45 ] so quickly.

答え: 3. spoken
hear O 過去分詞 で「Oが〜されるのを聞く」。English は speak される側なので過去分詞。

問15 (46)

The question [ 46 ] at today's meeting is whether we should postpone the plan till next month.

答え: 3. to be discussed
不定詞の形容詞的用法。「今日の会議で議論されるべき問題」の意。question は discuss される側なので受動態。

問16 (47)

If our last batter had not finally hit a home run, our team would [ 47 ] the game.

答え: 1. have lost
仮定法過去完了の文。If S had done ..., S would have done ...「もし〜だったら、…だっただろうに」。ホームランを打ったので勝った（負けなかった）。

問17 (48)

Don't go too far out from the shore in that small boat. [ 48 ]

答え: 2. it could be dangerous.
could は「〜する可能性がある」という推量を表す。遠くへ行くと危険な可能性がある、という警告。

1993年度 (本試験)

問1 (49)

Do you know how [ 49 ] in the evening the show will begin?

答え: 1. early
How early ...? で「どれくらい早く…？」と時間を尋ねる。fast, quickly, rapidly は速度。

問2 (50)

A book is not always a good book just [ 50 ] it is written by a famous writer.

答え: 1. because
not ... just because ~ で「単に〜だからといって…ではない」。

問3 (51)

I feel [ 51 ] and sleepy in Mr. Brown's lessons, since he is always using the same material.

答え: 1. bored
人が「退屈させられる」と感じる場合は bored (過去分詞)。boring は「退屈させるような」（授業内容など）。

問4 (52)

I went back to my hometown for the first time [ 52 ] ten years.

答え: 3. in
for the first time in ten years で「10年ぶりに初めて」。期間を表す in。

問5 (53)

Although her parents had said "no" for a long time, they finally [ 53 ] her go to Europe alone.

答え: 3. let
let 人 原形不定詞 で「人に〜させてあげる、許す」。allow は allow 人 to do。

問6 (54)

I can't [ 54 ] that noise. It's driving me crazy.

答え: 3. stand
can't stand ~ で「〜を我慢できない」。put up with なら可。

問7 (55)

I suggested to Mary [ 55 ] with me to collect empty cans on the street, but she said she was too busy.

答え: 2. that she come
suggest that S (should) 原形。「〜することを提案する」。should は省略可能。

問8 (56)

Heavy industry is moving in the direction of more automated machines and [ 56 ] manpower.

答え: 3. less
manpower（労働力）は不可算名詞なので、量を表す less を使う。few は可算名詞に使う。

問9 (57)

You can use a large plastic bottle, [ 57 ] cut off, as a pot to grow young plants in.

答え: 4. with its top
with + O + C（過去分詞）の付帯状況。「その上部が切り取られた状態で」。

問10 (58)

A large proportion of [ 58 ] English-speaking people watch on TV is of American origin.

答え: 2. what
関係代名詞 what を含む節 (what English-speaking people watch on TV) が前置詞 of の目的語になっている。「英語圏の人々がテレビで見るもの」の意。

問11 (59)

The heavy rainfall has caused vegetable prices to rise daily [ 59 ] the last two months.

答え: 2. for
for the last two months で「ここ2ヶ月間」。期間を表す for。

問12 (60)

you are [ 60 ] to gain weight in summer than in winter because you tend to lose your appetite when it is hot.

答え: 1. less likely
be likely to do で「〜しそうである」。文脈から「夏は冬よりも太りにくい（可能性が低い）」となるので less likely。

問13 (61)

[ 61 ] children the way she does, Sue should become a teacher.

答え: 3. liking
分詞構文。Assuming/Considering she likes children ... のような意味。「彼女のように子供が好きなので、スーは先生になるべきだ」。

問14 (62)

I'll be surprised [ 62 ] an accident. He drives too fast.

答え: 1. if tom doesn't have
I'll be surprised if S doesn't ... で「もしSが…しなければ驚きだ（＝きっと…するだろう）」。トムは運転が速いので、事故を起こさなかったら驚きだ、という意味。

問15 (63)

You live near us, don't you? Would you like to [ 63 ] our taxi?

答え: 4. share
share a taxi で「タクシーを相乗りする」。

問16 (64)

More and more videos were being rented in Britain, yet the number of movie tickets sold [ 64 ] 81 percent from 53 to 96 million.

答え: 3. increased by
increase by ~ で「〜（差額）だけ増加する」。53から96へは約81%の増加。

問17 (65)

After six hours' climbing, we finally [ 65 ] the top of the mountain.

答え: 3. succeeded in reaching
succeed in doing で「〜することに成功する」。reach は他動詞なので succeed in reaching the top。
