センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1999

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全14問)

1999年度 (本試験)

問1

“How did you do on the test?" "I don't know. For some reason, they've put off [ 141 ] the results until the end of the month."

答え: 3. announcing
put off doing で「〜するのを延期する」。put off は動名詞を目的語にとる。

問2

“I don't see Tom. I wonder why he's late." "Well, he [ 142 ] his train, or maybe he overslept."

答え: 1. might have missed
might have done で「〜したかもしれない」。過去の出来事に対する推量。

問3

“Why are you shopping for a bicycle? Didn't you buy one just last month?" "Yes, but unfortunately [ 143 ] last week."

答え: 2. it was stolen
物が「盗まれる」場合は steal を使い、受動態にする (it = bicycle)。rob は人や場所から「奪う」意味。

問4

“I'm very sorry, but the manager isn't here yet. Shall I have her call you when she gets in?" "No, I'll call back. If I call again in an hour, do you think she [ 144 ] ?"

答え: 4. will have arrived
in an hour (1時間後) という未来の時点までに「到着してしまっているだろうか」と完了している状態を推測するので、未来完了形を使う。

問5

“I like my job, but I wish I made more money." "Me too. If I [ 145 ] I could buy a new car."

答え: 1. did
If I did (= made more money) ... 仮定法過去の文。現在の事実に反する仮定。「もし（もっとお金を稼いで）いたら、買えるのに」。

問6

“John [ 146 ] to like the fried rice." "He sure does! That's his third helping."

答え: 4. seems
seem to do で「〜するように思われる」。3回目のおかわりをしている様子から判断している。

問7

"Lots of people criticize her for working too slowly at the office." "I know, but [ 147 ] what people say about her, she always finishes her work before the deadline."

答え: 4. contrary to
contrary to ~ で「〜とは反対に、〜に反して」。人々が言うこととは反対に、締め切りは守る、という文脈。

問8

“I heard you were invited to the President's party." "Yes. I was surprised at how easy the President was [ 148 ]."

答え: 4. to talk to
It was easy to talk to the President. の the President が主語になった形 (tough構文)。talk to 人 なので、前置詞 to が必要。

問9

“You've been driving for hours. Shall I take [ 149 ] ?" "No, that's okay. I enjoy driving and I know you hate it, especially on the highway."

答え: 3. over
take over で「（仕事などを）引き継ぐ、交代する」。ここでは運転を代わること。

問10

I don't think I can meet you at six tomorrow night because we have a lot of extra work this month. Probably, [ 150 ].

答え: 1. I'll still be working
tomorrow night at six という未来の特定の時点で「まだ働いているだろう」と予想される進行中の動作を表すので、未来進行形が適切。

問11

Mr. Roberts did not choose any of the three jackets because he found [ 151 ] satisfactory.

答え: 4. none of them
3つ以上のものについて「どれも〜ない」と全否定する場合は none of ~ を使う。neither は2つのものについて使う。

問12

I heard you're planning to build a new house. Do you know how much [ 152 ] ?

答え: 3. it will cost
間接疑問文の語順。how much (疑問詞) + S (it) + V (will cost)。

問13

A [ 153 ] is a down and up movement of the head used in many countries to show that you agree with or understand something.

答え: 1. nod
nod は同意や理解を示す「うなずき」。shake は否定を示す「首を横に振ること」。pat は「軽くたたくこと」。wink は「ウィンク」。

問14

If you [ 154 ] a place to somebody, you let them use it as long as they regularly pay you a certain amount of money.

答え: 4. rent
rent は場所や物をお金を払って「借りる」、またはお金をもらって「貸す」。ここでは「貸す」の意味。borrow は無料で借りる。lend は無料で貸す。
