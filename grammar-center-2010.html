センター試験 文法語法クイズ 2010

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全10問)

2010年度 (本試験)

問1

“Have you handed in the English assignment?” “No, but I'm halfway [ 257 ] it.”

答え: 4. through
be halfway through ~ で「〜の半分まで終えている」。宿題の半分まで終わっている。

問2

I have to get my commuter pass renewed because it [ 258 ] tomorrow.

答え: 3. expires
expire は「（期限が）切れる、満了する」。定期券の期限が明日切れる。

問3

Is it possible for you to postpone today's meeting [ 259 ] next Wednesday?

答え: 4. until
postpone ~ until ... で「〜を…まで延期する」。

問4

I was talked [ 260 ] buying a big bar by my sister.

答え: 3. out of
talk 人 out of doing で「人を説得して〜するのをやめさせる」。ここでは受動態で「買うのをやめるよう説得された」。↔ talk 人 into doing 「人を説得して〜させる」。

問5

If I hadn't broken up with Hannah last month, I [ 261 ] going out with her for two years.

答え: 4. would have been
仮定法過去完了。If S had done ..., S would have done ...。「もし別れていなかったら、2年間付き合っていたことになっただろうに」。

問6

The puppy at the rescue center looked happy to have been [ 262 ] by the little girl.

答え: 4. chosen
to have been + 過去分詞 で完了形の不定詞の受動態。「（それ以前に）選ばれたこと」。

問7

“Why did Jack quit his job?” “He wanted to [ 263 ] his dream of opening his own café.”

答え: 4. realize
realize one's dream で「夢を実現する」。come true は夢などが「実現する」（自動詞）。

問8

In my school, when a [ 264 ] teacher is absent, another teacher teaches the class instead.

答え: 3. regular
regular teacher で「正規の教員、担任の先生」。

問9

“Going through all the steps to adjust the brightness of my computer screen is a real nuisance.” “Well, why don't you do it the easy way? Just press this key. That's the [ 265 ].”

答え: 1. shortcut
shortcut は「近道、簡単な方法、ショートカットキー」。

問10

After many years of war, the country has lost much of its power. [ 266 ], its influence should not be underestimated.

答え: 1. Even so
Even so は「たとえそうであっても、それでもなお」。前の文の内容を受けて、逆接の関係を示す接続副詞。
