センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1991

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全17問)

1991年度 (本試験)

問1

Does Kenji still have that book he [ 15 ] from the library?

答え: 2. borrowed
borrow A from B「BからAを（無料で）借りる」。

問2

His wife is in the hospital because she was [ 16 ] in a car crash.

答え: 4. injured
人が事故で「怪我をする」場合は injure。

問3

I could tell he was only [ 17 ] to read, because his book was upside down.

答え: 4. pretending
pretend to do「〜するふりをする」。

問4

Before the exam I [ 18 ] my memory by reading some old notes.

答え: 2. refreshed
refresh one's memory「記憶を新たにする」。

問5

When I missed the last bus, I had no [ 19 ] but to take a taxi.

答え: 1. choice
have no choice but to do「〜するしかない」。

問6

In spite of the [ 20 ] traffic, I managed to get to the airport in time.

答え: 1. heavy
heavy traffic「交通渋滞」。

問7

I don't know what this word means. I'll look it [ 21 ] in the dictionary.

答え: 4. up
look up A「Aを調べる」。

問8

We'll have to move that big desk; it's really [ 22 ] the way.

答え: 2. in
be in the way「邪魔になっている」。

問9

Let's ask the neighbors to [ 23 ] the dog while we're away.

答え: 3. look after
look after ~「〜の世話をする」。

問10

I've already had one bad experience buying goods by mail order and I don't want [ 24 ].

答え: 1. another
another (experience)。「もう一つの」。

問11

I'd rather have a room of my own, [ 25 ] small it may be.

答え: 1. however
however 形容詞/副詞 S may be「どんなに〜であっても」。

問12

[ 26 ] seems easy at first often turns out to be difficult.

答え: 3. what
関係代名詞 what を含む節が主語。「〜なこと」。

問13

The boys should not [ 27 ] the ice cream, but they did.

答え: 4. have eaten
should not have done「〜すべきではなかったのに」。

問14

I'm having my house [ 28 ] at the moment, so could you come over next Friday instead?

答え: 3. painted
have O 過去分詞「Oを〜してもらう」。

問15

This symphony is a real masterpiece. I think it's worth [ 29 ] over and over again.

答え: 2. listening to
be worth doing「〜する価値がある」。listen to ~ なので to が必要。

問16

The boy opened the window, although his mother told him [ 30 ].

答え: 3. not to
tell 人 not to do。「〜しないように言う」。to 以下が省略。

問17

My wife usually doesn't drink coffee at night, and [ 31 ].

答え: 3. neither do i
否定文への同意。「〜もまた…ない」。Neither + 助動詞 + S。
