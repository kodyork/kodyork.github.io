センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1992

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全17問)

1992年度 (本試験)

問1

What is he talkig about? It just doesn't make [ 32 ].

答え: 3. sense
make sense「意味をなす」。

問2

Susan gave up her job, so she has a lot of time to [ 33 ].

答え: 4. spare
time to spare「自由に使える時間」。

問3

She had a dream of becoming a movie star even though she didn't know how to [ 34 ] it.

答え: 4. realize
realize a dream「夢を実現する」。

問4

It's only a small house but it [ 35 ] my needs perfectly.

答え: 2. meets
meet one's needs「要求を満たす」。

問5

Could you lend me some money? I'm [ 36 ] of cash at the moment.

答え: 4. short
be short of ~「〜が不足している」。

問6

I've lived near the airport so long that I'm now [ 37 ] to the noise of the airplanes.

答え: 4. used
be used to ~「〜に慣れている」。

問7

[ 38 ] your financial help, we wouldn't be able to carry out our plan.

答え: 4. without
Without ~ (, S would ...)。仮定法過去。

問8

[ 39 ] taking the regular tests, we have to hand in a long essay.

答え: 4. in addition to
「〜に加えて」。

問9

I just can't see why he doesn't [ 40 ] his motorcycle, since he never rides it any more.

答え: 4. get rid of
「〜を処分する」。

問10

There are two reasons for our decision, and you know one of them. Now I'll tell you [ 41 ].

答え: 3. the other
2つのうちの「もう一方」。

問11

The firemen had trouble getting to the street [ 42 ] the houses were on fire.

答え: 3. where
関係副詞。先行詞 the street を修飾。

問12

Remember [ 43 ] I've just told you. It'll be very important when you grow up.

答え: 3. what
関係代名詞 what。「〜なこと」。Remember の目的語。

問13

My parents never allowed me [ 44 ] alone in the sea.

答え: 3. to swim
allow 人 to do「人が〜することを許す」。

問14

Kate speaks English very fast. I've never heard English [ 45 ] so quickly.

答え: 3. spoken
hear O 過去分詞「Oが〜されるのを聞く」。

問15

The question [ 46 ] at today's meeting is whether we should postpone the plan till next month.

答え: 3. to be discussed
不定詞の形容詞的用法。「議論されるべき問題」。受動態。

問16

If our last batter had not finally hit a home run, our team would [ 47 ] the game.

答え: 1. have lost
仮定法過去完了。

問17

Don't go too far out from the shore in that small boat. [ 48 ]

答え: 2. it could be dangerous.
could「〜する可能性がある」。
