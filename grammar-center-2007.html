センター試験 文法語法クイズ 2007

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全11問)

2007年度 (本試験)

問1

After you pay a bill, you are given a [ 226 ] to show that you have paid.

答え: 4. receipt
receipt は「領収書、レシート」。支払いの証明として渡されるもの。

問2

Brett has many spare-time [ 227 ]; he swims, paints, plays the violin, and so on.

答え: 2. activities
activities は「活動」。水泳、絵画、バイオリン演奏などは余暇の活動。

問3

Small children have teeth which usually fall out between the ages of five and twelve, after which they get their [ 228 ] teeth.

答え: 4. permanent
permanent teeth で「永久歯」。乳歯 (baby teeth / milk teeth) が抜けた後に生えてくる歯。

問4

If you have an unreasonable dislike of someone, you are [ 229 ] that person.

答え: 4. prejudiced against
be prejudiced against ~ で「〜に対して偏見を持っている」。理由のない嫌悪感は偏見。

問5

My sister is in the front row in the picture. She is the one [ 230 ] in her hands.

答え: 4. with nothing
the one with nothing in her hands で「手に何も持っていない人」。

問6

After the car hit the boy, he [ 231 ] unconscious for two days.

答え: 1. remained
remain + 形容詞 で「〜のままでいる」。remain unconscious で「意識不明のままでいる」。

問7

It [ 232 ] for Mary to learn that her bike had been stolen.

答え: 4. was shocking
It is shocking (for 人) to do ... で「（人にとって）〜することは衝撃的だ」。形式主語 It に対する補語なので shocking (現在分詞)。

問8

My sister insisted [ 233 ] the problem by myself.

答え: 3. on my solving
insist on (one's) doing で「（人が）〜することを主張する」。on は前置詞なので後ろは動名詞。

問9

You should write down Satsuki's number [ 234 ] you forget it.

答え: 1. in case
in case S + V で「SがVする場合に備えて」。忘れる場合に備えて番号を書き留めるべきだ。

問10

My friend is going to fly back to California [ 235 ] Hawaii.

答え: 2. by way of
by way of ~ で「〜経由で」。= via。

問11

Please have [ 236 ] at this document before you begin your work.

答え: 1. a closer look
have a look at ~ で「〜を見る」。closer は look を修飾する形容詞。「もっとよく見てください」。
