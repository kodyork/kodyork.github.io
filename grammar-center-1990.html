センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1990

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全14問)

1990年度 (本試験)

問1

Look! There's a dog in the hall. Someone must have left the door [ 1 ].

答え: 2. open
leave O C (OをCの状態にしておく)。形容詞の open。

問2

Each of the sumo wrestlers [ 2 ] over 100 kg.

答え: 3. weighs
Each of ~ は単数扱い。動詞 weigh に三単現のs。

問3

After a lot of problems she [ 3 ] to learn to drive a car.

答え: 2. managed
manage to do「なんとか〜する」。

問4

I couldn't meet him at the station because my car ran out of [ 4 ].

答え: 1. gas
run out of ~「〜を使い果たす」。

問5

My TV set is almost 15 years old, but it still has a good [ 5 ].

答え: 3. picture
have a good picture「映りが良い」。

問6

This river is dangerous to [ 6 ] in July.

答え: 2. swim in
It is dangerous to swim in this river. の形。in が必要。

問7

[ 7 ] his many hours of study, he did not make much progress in German.

答え: 3. despite
「〜にもかかわらず」。前置詞。

問8

John and Mary [ 8 ] each other since 1976.

答え: 2. have known
know は状態動詞。since があるので現在完了形。

問9

His mother [ 9 ] to be more careful in his choice of words.

答え: 1. advised him
advise 人 to do「人に〜するように忠告する」。

問10

The Olympic Games were a huge success because so many countries [ 10 ].

答え: 1. participated
participate (in ~)「参加する」。自動詞。

問11

Where in Australia [ 11 ]?

答え: 1. did you grow up
grow up は自動詞「育つ」。

問12

Some people fear that World War III may break out soon, but I [ 12 ].

答え: 2. doubt if it will
doubt if/whether ~「〜かどうか疑う」。

問13

If you buy this, I will give you a [ 13 ].

答え: 1. fifteen percent discount
数値+percent が形容詞的に名詞を修飾。percent は複数形にしない。

問14

Jeff and Jenny saved [ 14 ] they could to visit their uncle in Hawaii.

答え: 2. as much money as
as much 名詞 as S can。「できるだけ多くの〜」。money は不可算名詞。
