センター試験 文法語法クイズ 1995

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全15問)

1995年度 (本試験)

問1

“This is my new dog. His name is Wisdom." "That's interesting. Why [ 83 ] did you give him such a name?

答え: 3. on earth
Why on earth ...?「一体全体なぜ…？」。強調。

問2

“Can you [ 84 ] what that sign says?" "Yes. From where I'm standing it's quite clear. It says 'No Smoking.'"

答え: 3. make out
「〜を理解する、判読する」。

問3

“Do you like that song?" "Yes, I do. It always [ 85 ] me of my happy school days."

答え: 4. reminds
remind A of B「AにBを思い出させる」。

問4

“Was Jack at the party?" "I don't think so. If he had been, I [ 86 ] him."

答え: 4. would have seen
仮定法過去完了。

問5

“Will you go shopping this afternoon?" "No, and [ 87 ]."

答え: 3. neither will mary
否定文への同意。「〜もまた…ない」。Neither + 助動詞 + S。

問6

“You're not afraid of insects, are you?" "No, certainly not. Not flies, not mosquitoes, and [ 88 ] ants."

答え: 4. least of all
「とりわけ〜ない」。

問7

It's [ 89 ] a long time since I started to teach at this school.

答え: 3. quite
quite a long time「かなり長い間」。

問8

She is so [ 90 ] that she cried for days when her pet rabbit died.

答え: 4. sensitive
「感受性の強い、敏感な」。

問9

We have [ 91 ] to walk before sunset.

答え: 2. another ten miles
another + 数詞 + 複数名詞。「さらに〜の」。

問10

There are few places downtown for parking, [ 92 ] is really a problem.

答え: 3. which
関係代名詞 which の非制限用法。先行詞は前文全体。

問11

I liked his new house, but I hadn't expected it [ 93 ] so small.

答え: 3. to be
expect O to be C。

問12

[ 94 ] fashion, I know nothing.

答え: 2. When it comes to
「〜のこととなると」。

問13

You will find the word "psychology" [ 95 ] under "P" in your dictionary.

答え: 3. listed
find O C（過去分詞）。「Oが〜されているのを見つける」。

問14

Our doctor always talks to me [ 96 ] a teacher talking to a child.

答え: 2. like
前置詞 like「〜のように」。

問15

The word [ 97 ] means money to be paid by people or businesses to a government for public purposes.

答え: 3. tax
「税金」。
