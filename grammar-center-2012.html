センター試験 文法語法クイズ 2012

空欄に入れるのに最も適当なものを一つ選べ。(全10問)

2012年度 (本試験)

問1

Some companies have [ 277 ] a new policy of using English as the official in-house language.

答え: 3. adopted
adopt a policy で「方針を採用する」。

問2

Could you show me how to make my mobile phone ring differently, [ 278 ] who's calling me?

答え: 1. depending on
depending on ~ で「〜に応じて、〜次第で」。電話をかけてくる人に応じて着信音を変える。

問3

Ms. Bell is stuck in a traffic jam. The important meeting will have finished by the time she [ 279 ].

答え: 1. arrives
by the time S' + V' ... (〜する時までには)。時を表す副詞節の中では、未来のことであっても現在形を使う。

問4

We had the microwave, the toaster and the heater all [ 280 ] at the same time, and the circuit breaker switched off.

答え: 2. on
have O on で「O（電化製品など）をつけている」。ここでは3つの電化製品すべてが「ついている」状態。

問5

Mr. Brown looked over the cliff and found he was standing at the edge of a [ 281 ] drop.

答え: 4. vertical
vertical drop で「垂直落下、（切り立った）崖」。cliff (崖) から連想される。

問6

You can apply for this overseas program on the [ 282 ] that you have a letter of recommendation from your teacher.

答え: 1. condition
on (the) condition that ... で「…という条件で」。

問7

“I heard Daiki's sisters are twins. Have you met them?” “No, I haven't met [ 283 ] of them yet.”

答え: 2. either
否定文で either of + 複数名詞 で「（2者のうち）どちらも〜ない」。neither は肯定文で使う。

問8

You should not let your personal emotions [ 284 ] in the way of making that important decision.

答え: 1. stand
let + O + 原形不定詞 で「Oに〜させる」。stand in the way of ~ で「〜の邪魔をする」。

問9

“Which girl is Shiori?” “The one [ 285 ] had a chat with a moment ago.”

答え: 1. I
The one (whom/that) I had a chat with ... の関係代名詞（目的格）が省略された形。「私が少し前に話した人」。

問10

After he joined the travel agency, he worked hard to improve his English in order to carry [ 286 ] his duties more effectively.

答え: 4. out
carry out ~ で「〜を実行する、遂行する」。職務 (duties) を遂行する。
